Photographs of the Canal Around Marsden

I live in the village of Marsden, up in the Pennines, about halfway between Manchester and Leeds.

The Huddersfield Narrow Canal passes through Marsden, just before it enters the Standedge Tunnel, the highest canal tunnel in England. Because Marsden is a small village, most of the walking is through the open countryside, and is very, very peaceful (most of the time). It’s a wonderful place to try and think through programming problems and design issues. Some of my best ideas have come to me while walking the tow path or sitting on a lock gate.
